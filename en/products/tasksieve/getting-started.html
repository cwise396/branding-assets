TaskSieve — Getting Started

Be ready in minutes: demo inbox, prioritization insights, connect your own sources.

1) Quick start (2–3 minutes)

  1. Open the TaskSieve page and click Start demo.
  2. Load the demo inbox (sample emails, notes, Slack snippets).
  3. Review prioritization: Impact • Urgency • Effort → Next action.

No import required. You can connect your sources later.

2) Connect your sources

  • Email: IMAP/Google Workspace (read-only is enough)
  • Notes: Markdown/notes export
  • Slack: channel read permissions

Contact

3) Working with TaskSieve

  • Next action first: ship the top 3 items first.
  • Focus blocks (2×25 min): batching reduces context switching.
  • Fine-tune: adjust weights when needed.

Tips & best practices

  • Mark “today” → visible momentum.
  • Weekly 10-min review: check sources, tweak rules.
  • Team start: begin small, then scale.
